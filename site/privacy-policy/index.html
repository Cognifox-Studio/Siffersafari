Siffersafari

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Overview

Siffersafari is a mathematics game app designed for children ages 6-12. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use the app.

Important: We do not collect, use, or disclose personal information from children or their parents. All gameplay data is stored locally on your device.

Data We Collect

We collect no personal information such as:

  • Child name, email address, postal address, or phone number
  • Parent contact details
  • Photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • Location data
  • Persistent device identifiers used for tracking

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Data type Purpose Stored where
Child profile name Identify the user in the app Local device storage via Hive
Quiz scores and results Track learning progress Local device storage via Hive
Progress and unlocked content Preserve learning progression Local device storage via Hive
Parent PIN (hashed) Control access to parent mode Local device storage via Hive, protected with BCrypt hashing
Session history Show cumulative statistics Local device storage via Hive

All data remains on your device. We do not sync data to our servers.

Data Not Collected

  • No server uploads of child or parent data
  • No analytics platforms such as Google Analytics, Firebase, or Mixpanel
  • No advertising, ad networks, or behavioral advertising
  • No tracking across apps or websites
  • No third-party data sharing

How You Control Your Data

Delete a Child Profile

  1. Open the Siffersafari app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select the child profile
  4. Choose Delete Profile
  5. Confirm deletion

Profile data, quiz history, and progress are permanently deleted from the device.

Clear All Data

  1. Open the Siffersafari app
  2. Go to Settings and then Advanced
  3. Choose Clear All Data
  4. Confirm deletion

All profiles, scores, and settings are erased. This action cannot be undone.

Data Security

  • Parent PINs are hashed using BCrypt and are not stored in plain text
  • Gameplay data is stored locally on the device
  • No network transmission is used for profile or progress data
  • No account system is required to use the app

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Siffersafari is designed for children under 13. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by collecting no personal information, storing data locally only, providing easy deletion, and avoiding tracking or profiling across devices or apps.

Third-Party Services

The app uses offline open-source libraries on the device only:

  • Hive for local database storage
  • Riverpod for state management
  • bcrypt for PIN hashing

None of these libraries are used to upload user data or track users.

Data Retention

  • Child profile data is kept until you delete the profile
  • Parent PIN data is kept until you delete the profile
  • Temporary files are cleared during normal app updates

Because the app has no centralized database, deletion is local and immediate.

Your Rights

  • Know what data the app stores locally
  • Delete your child's profile and associated data at any time
  • Ask questions about our privacy practices

Changes to This Policy

If our data practices change, we will update this policy and provide the new version with the app and its store listing.
